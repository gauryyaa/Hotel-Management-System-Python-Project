About Us

Launched in 1969, Royal Stays is a luxury hotel brand from INDIA.

RoyalStays Hotels

Enabling authentic indigenous experiences in harmony with environment & society Each RoyalStays Hotel is an archetype of the culture and ethos of the region that it is located in. The concept of each of the hotels weaves in the strongest elements of the culture of the region – harking back through architecture, interiors, art and artefacts to the major dynasties who have ruled India.
The unique value propositions, including the differentiated character of hotels, coupled with indigenous experiences is what makes every RoyalStays Hotel unique.
India’s centuries-rooted sacred relationship with its environment, of sustenance and nurture, is a vital and living heritage that is the cornerstone of the RoyalStays Hotels ethos and an affirmation of Responsible
Luxury: luxury without compromising the earth and sustainability without compromising luxury.
Responsible Luxury is the effective combination of luxury and responsible practices (enriching society and the environment) so that the luxury experiences delivered at RoyalStays Hotels do not in any way burden the environment or the society around us. In fact, it is aimed at creating a positive impact on both the community-at-large and the environment.
It ensures that sustainable practices are built into our hotels in the design phase itself, thus enabling RoyalStays Hotels to be eco-embedded, services to be eco-easy and our associates to be eco-sensitised; all of this while ensuring you indulge guilt-free.
All RoyalStays Hotels are LEED Platinum certified.
